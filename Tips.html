Tips & Tricks

Here are some useful tips to make your Batocera experience even better!

Speed Up Emulation

Turn off shaders and use lower resolution to make older systems run smoother.

Save Game States

Press F2 or your controller's hotkey + save button to quickly save progress!

Organize Your Games

Use folders by console to keep everything neat and easy to browse.

Backup Before Updating

Always copy your userdata folder before upgrading to avoid losing saves or settings.

Learn Hotkeys

Press Start + A in a game to see the hotkey list. It makes gameplay easier!
